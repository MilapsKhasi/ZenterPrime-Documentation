# User Guide for ZenterPrime Beta

Getting Started :
-------------------------------------------------------------------
Get started by setting up your ZenterPrime Beta account.

1. Starting with opening a web browser, choose any you like or use the most.
2. Open your browser and visit: "https://zenterprime-beta.vercel.app".
3. Click on **Sign Up** to create your ZenterPrime Beta account.
4. Enter your preferred email address and password.
5. Click on **+ New Workspace** or **Create New Workspace** based on your Beta version.
6. Enter your **Company Name**, **GSTIN**, and lastly **Address** for creating your new company.
7. Click on the workspace card that displays your company name.

Create your First Purchase Order : 
-------------------------------------------------------------------
To create purchase order & bill, follow the steps given below.

1. Click on *Purchase Bills* navigation button to view your Purchase register.
2. Now click on that **Create New Purchase** or **New Purchase Entry** button on the top right.
3. Enter your purchase bill details using the details from your supplier's purchase bill.
4. Save your entry and it will be appear in your Purchase bills table.
5. Return to the Dashboard. After it refreshes, your purchase total will be updated automatically.
6. You can also go to items and vendors tab to see the items and vendors have created automatically.

Create your First Sales Invoice : 
-------------------------------------------------------------------
To create sales invoice, follow the steps given below.

1. Click on *Sales Invoices* tab from navigation to open Sales Register.
2. Click on that **Create New Sales** button on the top right.
3. Enter the customer details, invoice information, and items.
4. Click on **Save** to save that bill in your register.
5. Click on **Save & Print** to save and also print that invoice and download it by selecting "Save as PDF".
6. Click on **Print Invoice** to preview your invoice before saving.
7. Go to dashboard and let the dashboard reloads, it will show the amount of the sales invoice in cards.
8. You can also go to items and customers tab to see the items and vendors have created automatically.

Generate Party Ledgers : 
-------------------------------------------------------------------
To view Customer's ledger, follow the steps given below.

1. Click on *Customers* tab from navigation.
2. Click on **Ledgers** button in the top right.
3. The popup window will open and you can view the complete ledger for the selected customer.
4. Click on **Download PDF** to download ledger.

To view Vendor's ledger, follow the steps given below.

1. Click on *Vendors* tab from navigation.
2. Click on **Ledgers** button in the top right.
3. The popup window will open and you can show the complete ledger of vendor.
4. Click on **Download PDF** to download ledger.

Create your Daily Cashbooks : 
-------------------------------------------------------------------
To create your cashbooks, follow the steps given below.

1. Click on *Cashbooks* tab from navigation.
2. Click on **New Cashbook** from top right.
3. Enter the date by clicking the date.
4. Enter the Opening Balance by clicking on 0.00 amount text.
5. Enter your income entries.
6. Enter your expense entries.
7. Check below and you'll see the total of Income, Expense and Closing Balance.
8. Click on **Save** to save the cashbook.

💡 Tip : Click on New Cashbook button again and you'll see the opening balance of that cashbook carried forward from previous book's closing balance.

Create GST-enabled Invoices : 
-------------------------------------------------------------------
Before creating GST invoices, enable GST for your company. Follow the steps to enable GST column in your invoices.

1. Go to *Settings* and scroll a bit down, you'll see a toggle button called 'Enable GST', turn that toggle to ON.
2. Keep the drop-down selected to CGST, SGST or change it to IGST before you go to create your sales invoice that supplies to other state.

Now create CGST, SGST, or IGST slabs, follow the steps given below.

1. Click on *Duties & Taxes* tab from navigation.
2. Click on **New Ledger** from top right.
3. Enter the *Ledger Name*, select the *Type* to **Change**, select *Calculation At* to **Percentage**.
4. Enter the *Default Value* to your prefered tax for e.g 5%, 12&, 18% etc.
5. Click on **Save** to save the ledger.
6. Enable the checkbox beside the tax ledger to make it available while creating invoices.
